Research Methods and Professional Practice

This course provides students with a practical basis in research methodologies, allowing them to undertake research in real-world contexts.

Learning Outcomes

After completing this module, students are expected to do the following:

  • Evaluate the professional, legal, social, cultural, and ethical challenges affecting computer experts
  • Examine academic inquiry concepts by applying them to a research problem in the relevant computer domain
  • Critically analyse the current literature, study design, and technique for the selected issue, as well as data analysis methods
  • Produce and justify a research proposal for the specified subject
